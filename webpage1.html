Ernakulam Terminus railway station

Ernakulam Terminus (Station Code:ERG), also called as Ernakulam Goods Station or Ernakulam Old Station, is an abandoned railway station in the Indian city of Kochi, Kerala.

Marine Drive Kochi

Marine Drive Kochi is a picturesque promenade that stretches for about 3 km along the shore of Vembanad Lake, one of the largest lakes in India. It is built facing the backwaters, which are a network of canals, lagoons, and rivers that connect the coast with the inland regions of Kerala. Marine Drive Kochi is a popular hangout for the local populace as well as tourists who visit Kochi for sightseeing, shopping, or relaxation.
